# Urban Survey Review Defaults

## Scope

Apply these defaults to review drafts about 도시개발사업, 실시설계, 지형현황측량, 측량 범위, and similar land-development survey topics.

## Drafting Defaults

- Start with `검토목적` before arguing the conclusion.
- Separate `법정 기준` from `실무 관행`.
- Do not state `20~50m` as a universal legal minimum.
- Treat `20~50m` as an initial review width or practical starting range for ordinary boundary segments.
- Explicitly say that connection zones, drainage influence zones, slope zones, and structure zones may need wider measurement coverage.
- Prefer “타당함”, “판단됨”, “보기 어려움” over overconfident legal language unless a cited rule is explicit.

## Preferred Logic

1. Explain why the scope matters at the design stage.
2. Review what the formal rule says and what it does not say.
3. Explain why field practice often uses extra width.
4. Present a differentiated scope-setting rule instead of one fixed number.
